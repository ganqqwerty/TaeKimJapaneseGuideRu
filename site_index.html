Grammar Index

The 【あ行】

  • ある－Verb of existence used for inanimate objects
  • いる－Verb of existence used for animate and/or living objects

The 【か行】

  • 「か」 Question Marker－Sentence ending marker used to ask questions and to talk about undecided matters
  • 「が」 Conjunctive －A conjunctive used to express a contradiction between two simple sentences
  • 「が」 Particle－Subject particle, identifies who or what is doing the action
  • 「から」 Conjunctive－A conjunctive used to express a reason for something.
  • カタカナ－A variant of the phonetic alphabet for foreign words
  • 漢字（かんじ）－Chinese characters adapted for Japanese
  • 「けど」 Conjunctive －Similar to 「が」, this is a more casual conjunctive used to express a contradiction between two simple sentences

The 【さ行】

  • 「し」 Conjunctive－A conjunctive used to list multiple reasons for something

The 【た行】

  • 「～たりする」 －A conjunctive for verbs and state of being used to list multiple reasons for something
  • Te-form－A type of verb conjugation used in many different types of grammar. It, in and of itself, is used to express a sequence of more than one action.
  • 「で」 Particle－Context particle used to express the means of which an action was performed
  • 「と」 Particle－Particle used to denote "and" used for nouns
  • 「とか」 Particle－Casual version of the 「や」 particle used to list a sample of items from a larger list

The 【な行】

  • 「に」 Particle－Target particle used to denote a target of a directional action
  • 「ね」－A sentence ending used to confirm an expectation of agreement
  • 「の」 Particle－Particle used to indicate possession, as a generic noun substitution, or to provide further explanation
  • 「ので」 Conjunctive－Similar to 「から」, this is a politer conjunctive used to express a reason for something.
  • 「のに」 Conjunctive－A conjunctive used to connect two sentences with the meaning, "in spite of", or "despite".

The 【は行】

  • 「は」 Particle－Topic particle, denotes a general topic (pronounced as / wa /)
  • ひらがな－The basic phonetic alphabet
  • 「へ」 Particle－Directional particle used to denote the destination of a motion verb (pronounced as / e / )

The 【ま行】

  • 「も」 Particle－Inclusive topic particle, denotes an additional general topic

The 【や行】

  • 「や」 Particle－Particle with the meaning "and"; it is used for nouns implying a sample of items from a larger list
  • 「よ」－A sentence ending used to inform the listener of new and previously unknown information
